About Basilia

Where Expertise
Meets Dedication

Basilia Azcona is a distinguished real estate professional serving the Central New Jersey market with an unwavering commitment to excellence. Rooted in Mercer County, she brings a deep understanding of the local landscape — from Princeton's storied neighborhoods to the vibrant communities of Hamilton, Trenton, and beyond.

As a bilingual English/Spanish agent, Basilia bridges cultures and opens doors for a diverse clientele, ensuring every client feels heard, understood, and expertly guided through one of life's most significant decisions. Her approach is personal, strategic, and relentlessly client-focused.

With over a decade of experience and more than $20 million in closed transactions, Basilia has earned her reputation as one of Central New Jersey's most trusted and results-driven real estate professionals.
